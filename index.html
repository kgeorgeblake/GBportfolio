About Me

George Blake is Postdoctoral Scholar in the Baker-Nord Center for the Humanities at Case Western Reserve University. Dr. Blake holds a PhD from the Department of Music at the University of California, Santa Barbara. His research focuses on the hidden musical histories of Cleveland, while examining how performers throughout the region negotiate the invisible borders of urban space. His ongoing work in the digital humanities is oriented around enriching research methods, extending music education and promoting digital literacy.
